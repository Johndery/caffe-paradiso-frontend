Our Story

A hidden gem on
Elizabeth Street

Born from the team behind Dante — one of NYC's most beloved bars — Caffe Paradiso brings that same obsessive craft to coffee. Behind a custom green onyx stone bar, we serve drinks that feel like small acts of devotion.

A few bar seats. A park across the street. And the best latte you'll ever have. Dreamers welcome.
